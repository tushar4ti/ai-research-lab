Who We Are

About The Laboratory

A premier center of excellence driving innovation through cutting-edge artificial intelligence research.

Bridging Academic Excellence
& Industry Innovation

The AI Research Laboratory at Daffodil International University is a bold convergence of curiosity-driven science and mission-oriented engineering. Since 2020 we've grown into an international hub where faculty, students, and industry partners co-design AI systems that are robust, ethical, and built to deliver measurable impact across society.

Backed by GPU-scale compute, advanced robotics platforms, and human-centered evaluation facilities, we translate ideas into real-world deployments. We publish open, reproducible research, forge industry collaborations, and mentor the next generation of AI leaders — all focused on solutions that uplift healthcare, sustainability, and inclusive economic growth.

World-Class Infrastructure HPC clusters with NVIDIA A100 GPUs
Global Collaborations Partnerships with MIT, Stanford, Oxford
Industry Integration Real-world impact through R&D partnerships
5+ Years of
Excellence

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as a globally recognized center of AI excellence that produces transformative research while nurturing ethical technology leaders who will shape the future of intelligent systems worldwide.

Our Mission

We advance the theoretical foundations of AI while developing practical, deployable solutions that create positive societal impact through rigorous research, quality education, and meaningful cross-sector collaboration.

Research Goals

Publish in top-tier venues (NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR), secure competitive grants, develop open-source tools, mentor graduate students, and translate research into products benefiting local communities and global markets.

Meet The Research Team

Director
Professor & Founding Director

Professor Dr. Marcus Chen

A distinguished researcher with over 18 years of pioneering experience in machine learning, pattern recognition, and computational intelligence. Published 90+ papers in top-tier venues including NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR, and AAAI. Recipient of the prestigious National Science Award for AI Innovation (2023). Holds a Ph.D. from Stanford University.

Under his leadership, the AI Research Lab has secured over $2 million in research funding, established partnerships with leading global institutions, and produced groundbreaking work in healthcare AI, autonomous systems, and natural language understanding.
